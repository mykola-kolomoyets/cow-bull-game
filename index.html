Bull-Cow game rules

You gave to guess the 4-digit number, which has unrepeatable digits
For example, 1234 is correct
And 1233 is incorrect
When you will enter the number, you will see the number of cows and bulls.

  1. Cow tells you then one of the digits is correct but its` position in the number is incorrect
  2. Bull means that both digit and it`s position are correct
